## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure that you have the following:

- A Zapier account. If you don't have one, you can sign up at [zapier.com](https://zapier.com/).
- Access to the LLM Radar's New Models RSS feed URL.
- Basic familiarity with creating Zaps in Zapier.

## Step 1: Create a New Zap

1. **Log in to Zapier:**
   - Open your browser and log in to your Zapier account.

2. **Create a New Zap:**
   - Click on "Create Zap" in the upper-left corner of the Zapier dashboard.

3. **Name Your Zap:**
   - Give your Zap a meaningful name, such as "Monitor LLM Radar New Models".

## Step 2: Set Up the RSS Trigger

1. **Choose the Trigger App:**
   - In the "Choose App & Event" section, search for "RSS by Zapier".
   - Select "RSS by Zapier" as the trigger app.

2. **Choose the Trigger Event:**
   - Select "New Item in Feed" as the trigger event.
   - Click "Continue".

3. **Set Up the Trigger:**
   - In the "Feed URL" field, enter the RSS feed URL:
     ```
     https://zapier.com/engine/rss/682420/llm-radar-new-models
     ```
   - If the feed requires authentication, click on "Show Options" and enter your username and password.
   - Optionally, set other parameters like "Limit Items" or "Time between polls".
   - Click "Continue".

4. **Test the Trigger:**
   - Zapier will fetch the latest item from the feed to ensure everything is working correctly.
   - Review the data retrieved and click "Continue" if it looks correct.
